About Us

Our Story & Mission

Empowering students through holistic education, expert mentorship, and mental wellness support.

Introduction

The Pragma Way

Pragma Education was founded by Bharat Bhushan Rallapalli, a former intelligence officer who believes education isn't just about information — it's about empowerment. He views true education as the realization and internalization of truths that inspire meaningful action.

Driven by this philosophy, Pragma Education stands as a comprehensive hub for examination aspirants, offering far more than just academic preparation. Our mission is to broaden perspectives, equipping students with the skills and understanding needed to navigate not just exams, but life itself.

At Pragma Education, we don't just prepare students for tests — we prepare them for success.

Overview

Our Journey

Pragma Education, established as Pragma Edu Solutions India LLP in July 2022, offers coaching for various competitive examinations related to entry into government and public services. We also provide a skills development module for all aspirants pursuing alternative career options.

To date, Pragma Education has provided online coaching to approximately 200 aspirants for the UPSC-CSE and Group-1 (AP & TS) exams. Additionally, we offer classes focused on developing various skills that contribute to essential life skills.

We are also committed to social responsibility, having delivered skills development programs for children in orphanages and juvenile justice homes through partnerships with NGOs.

Our Purpose

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To impart real education that is not emphasized in schools, colleges, or career jobs. Our purpose is to enhance the preparation journey experience of student aspirants, positively impacting their lives both during their preparation and beyond.

Our Mission

To help every student and aspirant identify their own SWOT and gain self-awareness. This journey provides crucial insights into the exams they will face, making all the difference in their success story.
