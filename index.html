About Us

Empowering Students Through Holistic Education

Pragma Education was founded by Bharat Bhushan Rallapalli, a former intelligence officer who believes education isn't just about information — it's about empowerment. True education is the realization and internalization of truths that inspire meaningful action.

Driven by this philosophy, Pragma Education stands as a comprehensive hub for examination aspirants, offering far more than just academic preparation. Our mission is to broaden perspectives, equipping students with the skills and understanding needed to navigate not just exams, but life itself.

  • Expert mentorship from ex-IB officers
  • Mental wellness integrated with preparation
  • Complete exam-focused coaching
  • Holistic development approach

Vision & Mission

Our Vision

To impart real education that is not emphasized in schools, colleges, or career jobs. Our purpose is to enhance the preparation journey experience of student aspirants, positively impacting their lives both during their preparation and beyond.

  • Mental wellness linked to UPSC preparation gap: 99.98% failure rate
  • Intense competition and pressure due to traditional methods of preparation
